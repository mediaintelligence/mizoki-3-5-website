Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "decision"?
A decision is any action that passes through the Decision Control Plane for authorization. This includes automated actions (budget adjustments, bid changes, inventory reorders) as well as recommendations that require human approval. Read-only queries and monitoring do not count against your decision quota.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can upgrade at any time and the new pricing takes effect immediately (prorated for the billing period). Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. Enterprise customers on annual contracts should discuss changes with their account team.
What happens if I exceed my decision limit?
We'll notify you at 80% and 90% of your limit. If you exceed your limit, decisions continue to process (we never block critical operations) but overage charges apply at $0.25/decision for Foundation, $0.15/decision for Professional. Enterprise plans have negotiated overage rates.
Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on Foundation and Professional tiers with full platform access and up to 5,000 decisions. No credit card required to start. Enterprise trials are scoped individually based on your requirements.
What's included in implementation support?
Foundation includes self-serve documentation, video tutorials, and community support. Professional includes guided onboarding sessions, data integration assistance, and a dedicated customer success manager. Enterprise includes full white-glove implementation with dedicated engineering resources.
Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits?
Yes, we offer 50% off Foundation tier for qualified startups (under $5M funding, less than 50 employees) and 30% off for registered non-profits. Contact our team to apply.
What's the contract length?
Foundation and Professional are available month-to-month or annually (15% discount for annual). Enterprise contracts are typically 1–3 years with volume commitments. All plans include a 30-day satisfaction guarantee.
