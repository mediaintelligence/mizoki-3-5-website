Core Innovations (Patent Pending)

Novel architecture enabling verifiable autonomous operation

Decision Control Plane (DCP)

Centralized authority that separates action proposal from authorization. Agents cannot bypass the DCP—this architectural constraint enables verifiable autonomy.

Validation & Arbitration Layer

Planner, Verifier, Risk, and Policy agents independently evaluate proposals. Disagreement is a signal, not an error—arbitration proceeds without requiring consensus.

Counterfactual Simulation Engine

Before execution, simulates proposed action, alternatives, and no-action baseline. Platform learns from simulated outcomes never executed.

Temporal-Causal Knowledge Graph

Decision memory and audit spine storing proposals, simulations, validations, and outcomes with timestamps, provenance, and causal confidence.
