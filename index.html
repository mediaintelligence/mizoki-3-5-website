01

SENSE — Ingest Live Signals

Connects to your existing ERP, CRM, warehouses, and cloud systems — plus market and operational feeds — without rip-and-replace. Builds situational awareness from what is already true.

ERP · CRM · Data Warehouse · Cloud
02

REASON — Evaluate Options & Causality

Uses knowledge graphs, causal modeling, and multi-agent reasoning to evaluate outcomes. Explains why outcomes occur and what will likely happen next — not just what happened.

Knowledge Graphs · Causal Modeling
03

PLAN — Create Action Strategies

Uses REWOO integration for step-by-step reasoning chains. Structured approach to complex problems with multiple viable paths evaluated.

REWOO · Step-by-Step Reasoning
04

VERIFY — Validate Before Execution

Devil's Advocate review challenges plans. Knowledge Graph constraint validation ensures compliance. Adversarial review catches blind spots before any action is taken.

Devil's Advocate · KG Validation
05

DECIDE — Score & Select Actions

Credibility-weighted MOA (Mixture of Agents) voting with formal arbitration protocol for consensus. Every decision is scored by confidence, risk, ROI, and historical outcomes.

MOA Voting · Confidence Scoring
06

ACT — Execute or Route for Approval

Executes decisions with pre-state capture for rollback. Autonomy is permissioned — actions run automatically or route to humans based on governance policy and risk thresholds.

Governed Execution · Rollback
07

LEARN — Update Models from Outcomes

Updates credibility scores, emits E-SHKG events, and continuously improves the system. LEARN is explicit — outcomes feed back into SENSE to improve future decisions. No manual retraining.

E-SHKG · Continuous Improvement

Why SRPVDAL?

MIZ OKI is not an AI assistant. The 7-stage pipeline ensures plans are verified before action, decisions are scored before execution, and outcomes feed back into improvement.
