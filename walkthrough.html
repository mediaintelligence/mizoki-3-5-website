Demo FAQ

How long is the demo?
The standard demo is 30 minutes: 10 minutes understanding your challenges, 15 minutes showing the platform, and 5 minutes discussing next steps. We can extend to 45-60 minutes for deeper technical dives.
Who should attend?
We recommend including the primary decision-maker and anyone who would use the platform daily. Common attendees include: VP/Director of Operations, Head of Marketing/Growth, CTO/Technical Lead, and Finance/Analytics leads.
Can I see a demo with my own data?
Yes! For qualified prospects, we can set up a proof-of-concept with your actual data. This typically happens after the initial demo and requires a brief technical discovery call to understand your data sources.
What do I need to prepare?
Nothing required, but it helps to have a rough idea of your annual revenue, number of decisions made monthly, current decision latency, and key pain points you're trying to solve.
Is there any obligation after the demo?
None whatsoever. We believe in demonstrating value without pressure. If MIZ OKI isn't the right fit, we'll tell you honestly. Our goal is to help you understand whether autonomous decision intelligence makes sense for your organization.
