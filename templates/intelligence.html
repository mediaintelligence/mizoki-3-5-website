Decision Control Plane

Centralized authority that authorizes all autonomous agent proposals. Agents propose, but the DCP authorizes. This separation enables governed autonomy.

Multiple independent agents verify every proposal before execution. Consensus-driven validation ensures no single point of failure in decision integrity.

Counterfactual Simulation

Before any action executes, the CSE simulates alternative outcomes. Decisions are stress-tested against possible futures, not just current conditions.

Every decision is recorded with full causal context. The TCO-KG serves as the immutable audit spine, enabling complete decision archaeology.
